Welcome to the game

To begin press the "Start Game" button below


Controls:

  • Movement: up/down/left/right : [w]/[a]/[s]/[d]
  • Pause Game: [Spacebar]
  • Restart Game - Only when dead: [r]

Difficulty:


Easy

Slower enemies but you gain less points


Normal

The intended difficulty to be played at


Hard

Much faster spawn rate, however there are more points available



This game is an arcade survival game to see how long you can survive.

Kill enemies, level up and grow more powerful as you try to beat your high score. Your score is increased after each enemy killed.
